Description

Jars of CBD Hemp Flower are a fantastic option for traditional cannabis smokers and those looking to use a pure CBD product in their own creations. CBD Flower can be used as a drop-in replacement for almost all cannabis edible recipes. You can even use it to make your own CBD butter!

What is CBD Flower?

CBD Flower is the all-natural plant material used to make CBD Oil products. However, unlike the hemp biomass that may be used to make CBD extracts, CBD Flower contains only the highest quality premium buds. CBD extracts may contain almost any part of the hemp plant, but our flower jars contain only the female flowers, scientifically known as Cola.

This makes flower, interchangeably also called bud, ideal for smoking and creating your own extracts, such as oil or butter. Our flower is hand-selected from growers across the country and is rich in terpenes and the found in hemp plants.

Our CBD buds test between 14 - 20% CBD, with less than 0.3% delta-9 THC.

How does CBD Flower differ from CBD Cigarettes and CBD Joints?

CBD Flower is the same plant material that goes into our fantastic CBD Joints, but CBD Cigarettes use a different, leafier shake material that allows the cigarettes burn more smoothly for an experience more similar traditional tobacco consumption. None of our products contain tobacco.

Jars of flower offer more flexibility than pre-rolled joints and may present more options for CBD consumers who do not wish to smoke CBD, but may be more interested in the unprocessed benefits that pure flower presents.
