OG Kush makes up the genetic backbone of many west coast cannabis varieties. When bred with a later OG Kush derivative, Pre98 Bubba, and an elite CBD cultivar, OG Kush Hemp was born. OG Kush is cherished for its ability to crush stress under the weight of its heavy cannabinoids and terpene profiles and Kush Hemp is no different.

Carrying grassy and sour lemon scents with woody undertones, Kush Hemp is a signature CBD strain with deep roots into legendary cannabis strains of the 1990s.
